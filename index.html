About

FDOA / Yoshiaki Yamaguchi

Outline

FDOA is an independent design office led by Yoshiaki Yamaguchi in Fukuoka, Japan. Projects range from small restaurants and shops to furniture, objects and collaborative architectural work.

Approach

Rather than chasing trends, the focus is on structure, proportion and how a space or object is actually used over time – finding forms that feel calm, clear and necessary.

Keywords

Interior architecture, furniture, product design, BIM / Vectorworks, hospitality, retail, everyday tools, long-term value.
